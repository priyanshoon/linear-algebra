\section{Introduction}
\subsection{Linear Equation}

\begin{itemize}
    \item A Linear Equation is an equation that can be written in the form
    \[ a_1x_1 + a_2x_2 + ... + a_nx_n = b \]
    \item The number $a_1, ..., a_n$ are called coefficients.
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection{Example}

\[ -x_1 + \pi x_3 - \sqrt[]2 x_4 = 0.007 \]
Here the coefficient of first term $x_1$ is $-1$, coefficient of $x_3$ is
$\pi$ and coefficient of $x_4$ is $\sqrt[]2$.
